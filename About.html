About

Chuan Hsieh was born in 1994. He lives in Taiwan with his family before moved to the United States in the 2000s. He went on explored many things before he find himself in the path of the visual artist when he was alway fascinated by the animations and motion movements that were made. He attended the University of Missouri in Columbia in order to learn more about arts and filming as he learned about what previous artists have achieved in the past. He learned about 3d arts with computers and practice quite number of things he could envisioned and learned from his professor.
